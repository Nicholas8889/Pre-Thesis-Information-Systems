CV TAJUK - REVENUE CYCLE MVP
CURRENT APPLICATION FEATURES
Updated: 19 June 2026

1. LOGIN AND USER IDENTITY
- Secure login and logout.
- Password field includes a Show/Hide icon so users can verify the entered password.
- Demo accounts for Manager, Admin, and Sales roles.
- The active user's name and role are visible in the application header/sidebar.
- Manager can view and use every current feature.
- Sales can view every module and create Sales Orders, but Invoice, Payment, Surat Jalan, and account creation controls are disabled.
- Admin can view every module and handle Invoice, Payment, Surat Jalan, Billing, and accounts, but Sales Order creation is disabled and Audit Trail remains read-only.
- Restricted controls stay visible in a disabled style and show a role explanation when hovered.

2. ROLE-SPECIFIC DASHBOARDS
- Manager: complete business overview, recent sales orders, revenue-cycle status, and sales/customer insights.
- Admin: compact recent sales orders plus operational insight into invoices, Surat Jalan, upcoming or overdue receivables, and Billing work that needs attention.
- Sales: customer-focused dashboard with overdue-payment customers, searchable customer categories, and recommended price markup.
- Manager dashboard shows the top 5 popular products by quantity sold in a compact horizontal bar chart.

3. NOTIFICATION CENTER
- Notification button is located beside the Help button.
- An orange dot appears when the current user has unread notifications.
- Admin receives reminders for Billing deadlines that are near or overdue.
- Sales receives reminders to follow up customers with no transaction in the last three months.
- Notifications show a short description and sent date, and can link to the related Billing or Follow Up page.
- Opening the notification panel marks the current notifications as read for that user.

4. CUSTOMER MANAGEMENT AND INTELLIGENCE
- View, search, add, and open customer records.
- Customer table includes category and payment-risk columns.
- Customer categories:
  * Loyal Customer: more than 3 transactions per month - recommended markup 0%.
  * Normal Customer: approximately 1 transaction per month - recommended markup 5%.
  * Occasional Customer: less than 1 transaction per month - recommended markup 10-15%.
  * New Customer: added less than 1 month ago - recommended markup 0%.
- Payment-risk indicators:
  * Late Payment: currently has an overdue unpaid receivable.
  * Historically Late: previously paid after the due date.
  * Clean: no detected late-payment history.
- Customer detail shows category and payment-risk summary cards.
- Customer detail lists every transaction with order number, order date, payment term, status, invoice, Surat Jalan, total, and a button to open the transaction.

5. SALES ORDER MANAGEMENT
- Create, view, search, filter, edit, delete, and print sales orders.
- Clean Sales-created orders stop at Confirmed so an Admin or Manager can generate the Invoice.
- Sales-created orders for customers with Late Payment or Historically Late risk require Manager approval before invoicing.
- A Need Approval tab lets Managers review transaction details, add an optional decision note, and approve or reject the order.
- Managers receive a notification when a new sales order needs approval.
- Approved orders generate their invoice and continue through the revenue cycle; rejected orders are cancelled.
- Manage order items, quantities, prices, discounts, taxes, payment terms, notes, and totals.
- Track order progress through Draft, Confirmed, Invoiced, and Cancelled statuses.
- Download Sales Order data as an Excel (.xlsx) file.
- Excel export opens a date-range popup with start-date and end-date calendars.
- Exported workbook contains Summary and Items sheets with formatted headings and filters.

6. INVOICE MANAGEMENT
- Create invoices from sales orders.
- View, search, filter, edit, delete, and print invoices.
- Track invoice status, issue date, due date, payment term, and totals.
- Identify sales orders that still need an invoice.

7. PAYMENT MANAGEMENT
- Record customer payments against invoices.
- View, search, filter, edit, and delete payment records.
- Track payment date, method, amount, and reference.

8. SURAT JALAN MANAGEMENT
- Create Surat Jalan records from sales orders.
- View, search, filter, edit, delete, and print delivery documents.
- Track delivery date, recipient, vehicle, notes, and status.
- Identify sales orders that still need a Surat Jalan.

9. RECEIVABLE MONITORING
- Monitor invoice totals, paid amounts, outstanding balances, and due dates.
- Highlight overdue and upcoming unpaid receivables.
- Show unpaid receivables when no overdue receivable exists.

10. BILLING
- Dedicated Billing menu for customer payment collection work.
- Create and review Billing tasks linked to customers and invoices.
- Record due date, assigned user, status, and notes.
- Dashboard and notifications highlight Billing tasks requiring Admin attention.

11. PRODUCT FOLLOW UP
- Dedicated Follow Up menu showing all customers.
- Shows the most recent product follow-up date for each customer.
- Search customers and identify customers that have never been contacted.
- Record contact date and an optional note about the conversation.
- Selecting Record Contact from a customer row automatically fills that customer in the contact form.
- Supports Sales reminders when customers have not ordered for three months.

12. AUDIT TRAIL
- Records important business actions and changes.
- Supports filtering by date, action, module, and user.
- Includes Sales Order, Invoice, Payment, Surat Jalan, Billing, and Follow Up activity.

13. REPORTING, PRINTING, AND HELP
- Dashboard summaries and status counts for revenue-cycle operations.
- Printable Sales Order, Invoice, and Surat Jalan documents.
- Contextual Help is available from the application header.
- Every operational table supports sortable columns and one simple table-wide search that shows rows containing the entered text in any field.
- Each meaningful column has a compact sort icon in its header.
- Funnel filters appear only for date columns or columns with meaningfully repeated values.
- Repeated-value filters open a popup with a value search and selectable checkboxes.
- Date-column filter popups provide Start Date, End Date, and a Start Filter button.
- Table filters include visible-result counts and a Reset action; print views remain unchanged.

14. DEMO DATA AND QUALITY CHECKS
- Seed data includes 100 generated customers with connected revenue-cycle activity, in addition to the original demonstration records.
- Generated data covers varied order approvals, Invoice statuses, Payments, Surat Jalan, Billing, product Follow Up, and Audit Trail entries.
- Automated tests cover access, dashboard behavior, customer intelligence, Billing, Follow Up, notifications, and Excel export.
- The project includes linting, type checking, production build validation, and database migrations.

CURRENT SCOPE
- This is an MVP focused on the revenue cycle: Customer -> Sales Order -> Invoice -> Delivery -> Payment -> Receivable -> Billing/Follow Up.
- Roles currently control the dashboard experience and reminders; full page-by-page permission restrictions are not yet implemented.
